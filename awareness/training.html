Accessible Procurement Training Sessions available in May 2026

Whether you are a federal procurement professional (contracting authority) or a business owner (technical authority), there are several accessible procurement training sessions coming this May. These sessions will help you to:

  • Build your capacity to consider and integrate accessibility into procurement specifications.
  • Prepare for the mandatory application of the national standard for information and communication technology (ICT) accessibility.
  • Find the resources and help that you will need!

NOTE: These sessions are offered three times per calendar year. Presentations are kept evergreen to remain current with developments in accessibility. Below is a list of the sessions on offer this coming May.

If you have any questions or require accommodation (such as ASL or LSQ interpretation services), please contact the AAACT Learning team by April 10, 2026 so we can make arrangements in advance.

How to consider and integrate accessibility in procurement

Are you a new federal procurement professional or a business owner who wants to learn more about integrating accessibility in federal procurement? Do not miss this joint PSPC and SSC presentation! Learn about key definitions, legislation and practical steps to ensure accessibility in your procurement process.

  • English session - Consider and integrate accessibility: May 5, 2026, from 1 to 2 pm (EDT)
  • French session - Considerer et integrer l'accessibilite : 6 mai 2026, de 13 h à 14 h (HAE)

Practical Guidance to Procuring Accessible Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

Learn how to incorporate the Government of Canada's recommended ICT accessibility standard when procuring ICT goods and services.

  • English session - Procuring accessible ICT: May 12, 2026, from 1 to 2 pm (EDT)
  • French session - Procuring accessible ICT: May 13, 2026, from 1 to 2 pm (EDT)

Practical Information on Understanding Accessibility Conformance Reports and Accessibility Roadmaps

Discover the key elements that business owners will need in order to understand Accessibility Conformance Reports and Accessibility Remediation Roadmaps.

  • English session - Conformance reports and roadmaps: May 26, 2026, from 1 to 2 pm (EDT)
  • French session - Conformance reports and roadmaps: May 27, 2026, from 1 to 2 pm (EDT)
