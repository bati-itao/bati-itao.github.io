Training and Coaching 2025

The purpose of the training is to develop and create accessible documents and applications as well as deepen our knowledge of adaptive technological methods used by people with functional limitations.

Did you know that many other learning opportunities are offered by College@ESDC? Use the Course Finder tool Internal (GC network) on iService to find those best suited for you! In the Course Finder subject field, select 'Diversity, Accessibility and Inclusion'. For more information or for accessible methods of registration please contact the College@ESDC: nc-college-gd@servicecanada.gc.ca.

To register for the class of your choice or to complete the training, participants must sign in to their Saba account Internal (GC network).

Don't have a Saba account? No problem! - just follow Instructions to access Saba account Internal (GC network) and register to the events.
