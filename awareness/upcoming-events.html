Upcoming Events

The following are awareness events offered by the IT Accessibility Office. You will have the opportunity to deepen your knowledge of content related to accessibility.

Please note that the sessions will be recorded and available in the Past Events.

Important!

Separate registration is required for each workshop, as you can attend Workshop 1 or Workshop 2 without having to participate in both.

January

Workshop 1 | Accessibility in Action: From Barriers to Better Documents

Want to make your communications truly inclusive?

Join us for an interactive bilingual workshop!

Learn how to identify accessibility barriers and create inclusive content in Outlook, Microsoft Word, and PowerPoint. This hands-on session, led by Wendy Morrissey, Operational Expertise Advisor at Service Canada, will provide you with practical tips and tools to make your communications more accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Register | January 21, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM (EST)

February

Workshop 2 | Accessibility in Everyday Communication: Hands-On Tips and Best Practices

Who should attend? Anyone who writes, edits, reviews, or produces content, no experience required. If you create documents, this session is for you! Learn how to make your documents accessible by working with real work examples to identify and fix common issues. As a bonus, you will experience how your document behaves with a screen reader, offering an eye-opening perspective on accessibility.

Register | 11 February 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM (EST)
