## Module Overview

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of document accessibility. You will learn what accessibility means, who benefits from accessible documents, and the basic principles that guide accessible design.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

- Define document accessibility and its importance
- Identify different types of disabilities that affect document access
- Understand key accessibility guidelines and standards
- Recognize relevant laws and government standards
