Text, fonts, and spacing

Fonts and size

  • Use common, readable fonts (e.g., Arial, Helvetica, Calibri, Verdana, Noto, Source Sans).
  • Body text size: at least 12–14 pt in documents; 18–24+ pt for slide content.
  • Avoid decorative fonts in body text; use them sparingly in headings if contrast is strong.
  • Avoid ALL CAPS and excessive italics; they reduce readability.

Line spacing and alignment

  • Line spacing around 1.2–1.5 improves readability.
  • Use left-aligned text; avoid full justification.
  • Use sufficient paragraph spacing rather than extra blank lines.

Emphasis and color

  • Don’t use color alone for meaning; pair with text or icons.
  • Ensure contrast meets WCAG thresholds (see the contrast lesson).
  • Use consistent styles for emphasis.

Check your work

  • Zoom to 200% to confirm content remains legible.
  • Print preview or export to PDF to check spacing consistency.
