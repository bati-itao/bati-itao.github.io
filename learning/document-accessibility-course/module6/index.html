Module 6 – Introduction to Accessible PDFs

This module provides an overview of what makes a PDF accessible, including tagging, reading order, and the use of accessibility checkers to verify compliance. You will learn about the fundamental principles of PDF accessibility, how to identify and resolve common accessibility issues, and the tools available to help ensure your PDFs are usable by everyone, including people using assistive technologies.
