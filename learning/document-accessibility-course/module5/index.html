Module 5 – Outlook Email Accessibility Techniques

This module covers best practices for creating accessible emails in Outlook, including email structure, clear subject lines, accessible content writing, headings and lists, images with alt text, descriptive links, and using Outlook's accessibility tools. You will learn how to compose emails that are accessible to users with disabilities, ensure proper organization and navigation within your messages, and leverage Outlook's built-in accessibility features. These techniques help ensure that your email communications are inclusive and accessible to all recipients.

  2. Clear and concise subject lines
  3. Writing accessible content for emails

  6. Descriptive links in emails
  7. Email attachments accessibility
  8. Accessibility checker in Outlook
  9. Email templates and best practices
  10. Best practice
