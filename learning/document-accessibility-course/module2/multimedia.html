Captions and transcripts

  • Captions for synchronized speech and significant sounds in video.
  • Transcripts for audio-only media and as a supplement to video.
  • Prefer human-reviewed captions; correct auto-captions for accuracy.

Audio description

Provide audio description when visual content is needed to understand the video (actions, on-screen text, charts).

Player accessibility

  • Ensure all controls are keyboard accessible and labeled.
  • Avoid autoplay; if unavoidable, provide an easy way to pause/stop.
  • Provide volume and caption toggles; expose focus indicators.

Embedding considerations

  • Provide a direct link to the media as a fallback.
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for player controls and captions.
