WCAG contrast ratios

  • Normal text: at least 4.5:1.
  • Large text (18 pt or 14 pt bold+): at least 3:1.
  • UI components/graphics: at least 3:1 against adjacent colors.

Test with a contrast checker (e.g., Colour Contrast Analyser, WebAIM Contrast Checker).

Don’t rely on color alone

  • Pair color with text, patterns, or icons.
  • Use labels or symbols in charts to differentiate series in addition to color.

Backgrounds and overlays

  • Avoid text over images. If necessary, use a solid or semi-opaque overlay to maintain contrast.
  • Check contrast for text in buttons, links, and navigation.

Check your work

  • Verify contrast for all text sizes and states (normal, hover, visited).
  • Print in grayscale to see if meaning is preserved without color.
