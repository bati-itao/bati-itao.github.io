Module 2: Contrast Accessibility Guidance

Ensuring adequate contrast between text and background colors is crucial in making content accessible to all users, particularly those with visual impairments. Here are key guidelines to follow:

  • Use a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text.
  • For large text (18pt and above), a contrast ratio of 3:1 is acceptable.
  • Test contrast using online tools or browser extensions to ensure compliance with WCAG standards.
  • Consider using colors that are distinguishable for color-blind users.
  • Provide options for users to adjust contrast settings if possible.

By adhering to these guidelines, you can improve the readability of your content and enhance the overall user experience.
